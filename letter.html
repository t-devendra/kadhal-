Somewhere in time

Today isn’t just your birthday.
It’s a reminder that the world once decided to place you in it and somehow, my life brushed past that decision.

I won’t pretend I know everything about you.
I don’t. I only know the version of you that stayed with me in silences, in half-spoken thoughts, in moments that didn’t ask to be named. And that was enough to change the way I look at things.

You taught me something without ever trying
that care doesn’t need possession, that closeness doesn’t always come from holding on, and that some feelings are meant to be carried quietly, like a fragile truth.

If I’ve ever loved you, it was never loudly.
It was in restraint.
In choosing not to ask for more. In letting you be exactly who you are, even when it meant standing a little apart.

I don’t know where life is taking you next, but I hope it is kind. I hope it gives you days that feel light, nights that feel safe, and people who never make you doubt your worth. And if somewhere along the way you remember me, I hope it’s gently without weight, without regret.

This isn’t a promise.
It isn’t a request.
It’s just the truth I allow myself today.

Happy Birthday Antra.
Always here.
